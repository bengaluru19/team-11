About Us

Our Mission Our goal is to create ONE MILLION entrepreneurs within five years of the program implementation. Through our Udhyam Shiksha Program (School level business clubs that learn and create real businesses), and Udhyam Vyapaar Program (Mentoring small businesses to scale and succeed), we look at helping people across urban low-paying/ rural economic sectors.

In this pursuit, we co-design and execute social interventions with a goal to increase Human Agency(capacity of individuals to act independently and to make their own free choices, based on their will) in our participant group primary of which are Youth Learners and Micro-entrepreneurs. Our Theory of Change is to facilitate entrepreneurial mindsets in our participant groups through two programmes Udhyam Shiksha and Udhyam Vyapar. We are a high energy team which believes in co-designing scalable solutions & a for-impact section 8 company working towards creating sustained social impact.
